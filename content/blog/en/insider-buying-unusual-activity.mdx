**What is a Form 4 filing and where can I find them?**

A Form 4 is a SEC filing required under Section 16 of the Exchange Act whenever a corporate insider buys, sells, or holds company stock.. It must be filed within two business days of the transaction. **Can insider buying be a reliable signal for stock market gains?**

Clustered insider buying by multiple insiders at the same company is a stronger signal than isolated purchases. The academic consensus treats it as bullish when filtered against stock-specific fundamentals and short interest. Signal strength rises when insiders buy with cash, not option exercises, and when the purchase is large relative to the insider's compensation..
