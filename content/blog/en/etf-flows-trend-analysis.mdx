ETF flows trend analysis examines the net investment dollars flowing into or out of exchange-traded funds, reflecting investor demand through changes in shares outstanding. This analysis is crucial for gauging investor sentiment (risk-on/off) and can be segmented by sector, strategy, or theme. However, accurate interpretation requires accounting for nuances like 'create to short' activities and the influence of model portfolios, which can distort raw flow data.

## What the data shows

- ETF flows measure the value of investment dollars added or withdrawn from an ETF.
- Net flow calculation reflects changes in shares outstanding based on investor demand.
- ETF flows can be used to assess investor risk-on/risk-off sentiment.
